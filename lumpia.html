Lumpia Recipe


Lumpia is a delicious Filipino spring roll typically filled with ground meat and vegetables, wrapped in thin spring roll wrappers, and deep-fried until crispy. Perfect as an appetizer or snack!

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground pork
  • 1 cup carrots, shredded
  • 1 cup cabbage, shredded
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 25-30 lumpia wrappers
  • Oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. Mix pork, carrots, cabbage, onion, garlic, egg, soy sauce, and pepper.
  2. Place filling on wrapper, roll, and seal with water.
  3. Fry in oil until golden. Drain excess oil.
  4. Serve with dipping sauce.
